About the Project

This web-based payroll management system was developed to streamline employee compensation processing for small to medium-sized businesses. It eliminates manual calculations and reduces errors in payroll processing.

The system provides a clean dashboard for HR personnel to manage employee records, compute salaries with automatic tax and deduction handling, and generate payslips in a single workflow.

Key Features

  • Employee records management with full CRUD operations
  • Automated salary computation with configurable deductions and benefits
  • Tax calculation based on government-mandated rates (SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG)
  • Payslip generation and printing per employee
  • Monthly and annual payroll reports with export options
  • Role-based access control for HR and admin users
